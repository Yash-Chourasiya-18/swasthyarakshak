💡 Mental Wellness Tips
🧘 Breathe: Try box breathing — inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s, hold 4s. Reduces anxiety instantly.
🚶 Move: Even a 10-minute walk can boost mood by releasing endorphins.
📵 Disconnect: Take 30-minute breaks from screens. Social media can amplify stress.
❤️ Connect: Talk to a friend or family member. Social support is crucial for mental health.
